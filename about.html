About Me

A life dedicated to public service and community development

Background

Senator Esther Okenyuri Anyieni is a nominated senator in the Republic of Kenya's Senate. She is a passionate advocate for education, healthcare, gender equality, and community development. Born and raised in Kenya, she has dedicated her entire career to uplifting communities and ensuring every Kenyan has access to the resources and opportunities they deserve.

Education

An alumni of Masai University and The Kenya High School, Senator Anyieni holds a degree in Information Science and has undertaken advanced studies in governance, leadership, and public policy. Her academic background informs her evidence-based approach to legislation and community engagement.

Vision for Kenya

"I believe in a Kenya where every citizen — regardless of background, gender, or geography — can access quality education, affordable healthcare, and economic opportunities. My work in the Senate is guided by the principles of integrity, transparency, and unwavering commitment to the people."
